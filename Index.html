Lesson 1: Ein Tag im Büro

German Text (Color Coded):

Der neue Manager gibt der intelligenten Frau das wichtige Dokument, und er erklärt schnell die Aufgabe, weil das Meeting bald beginnt.

English Translation:

The new manager gives the important document to the intelligent woman, and he quickly explains the task because the meeting will begin soon.

Key Vocabulary:

  • der Manager - the manager
  • die Aufgabe - the task
  • das Dokument - the document
  • wichtig - important

German Grammar Focus:

Sentence Structure: Main vs. Subordinate Clauses

This sentence shows how German sentence structure changes with different types of clauses, especially the verb position.

  • Main Clause (Hauptsatz): In the first part, "...der neue Manager gibt...", the conjugated verb ("gibt") is in the second position, which is standard for a main clause.
  • Subordinate Clause (Nebensatz): The connector "weil" (because) introduces a subordinate clause. In these clauses, the conjugated verb ("beginnt") is always moved to the very end of the clause.
